Navigating Hoofddorp

Hoofddorp is a vibrant city with a multitude of experiences waiting for you to explore. To make your journey easier, we provide essential information about the city.

🚎 Getting Around

  • Transportation: Hoofddorp boasts convenient public buses, trams, and trains that connect you to neighboring cities.
  • Biking: Embrace the city's bike-friendly culture with numerous paths and rental options available.
  • Driving: If you prefer driving, car rentals are accessible, and parking is readily available.

🔎Essential Services

  • Healthcare: Access modern healthcare facilities and pharmacies to ensure your well-being.
  • Education: Choose from a variety of international and local schools for your children's education.
  • Groceries: Shop conveniently at well-stocked supermarkets,such as Aldi, Lidl, Albert Heijin, Jumbo.
  • Community Support: Connect with expat organizations, enroll in language.
