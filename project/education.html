Education Opportunities in Hoofddorp

📘Hoofddorp offers different schools for kids of all ages from various cultural backgrounds, and we're here to help you understand your options.

Types of Schools:

  • Primary Schools: These are for young children, usually from ages 4 to 12.
  • Secondary Schools: For older kids, typically from ages 12 to 18.

Extracurricular Activities:

  • Some schools offer fun activities like sports, music, and art.

School Choices:

  • MBO College Airport-ROC van Amsterdam: an inclusive vocational institution that offers a variety of practical career-oriented programs, providing a welcoming environment for students from diverse backgrounds, including Islamic children.
  • Gifted Minds International School: provides educational services for preschool and primary school. Preschool Section ranges from 3 to 5 years old and primary Section ranges from 6 to 12 years old.
  • Optimist International School: a public primary school offering a happy, safe and encouraging place where everyone feels welcome and valued.

How to Choose a School:

  • You can visit schools to see if you like them and meet the teachers.
  • Talk to other parents in Hoofddorp for recommendations.

Help for Newcomers:

  • Language Support: If your kids are learning Dutch, some schools offer language support.
  • International Communities: Hoofddorp has a friendly international community to help you settle in.
